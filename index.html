About Robb Music Group

Robb Music Group Corporation is a New York-based record company, music publisher, and management group (talent are managed by Robb Talent Group) that produces, markets, and distributes music from various genres (pop, R&B, hip-hop, indie, punk, rock, heavy metal, etc.) and also manages the careers of musicians. Robb Music Group Corporation is owned by Robb Entertainment Corporation - robbent.com.
